Featured Speakers

Dr. Li Wei

Computational biologist

Dr. Li Wei is a computational biologist at Genomics Research Center, specializing in the application of AI to solve complex biological problems. His work has significantly contributed to the understanding of genomic data and disease prediction.

AI Engineer

AI scientist
